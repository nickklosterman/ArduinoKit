1 x LM35 sensor module - lm35 temperature sensor its just a small semicircular three pronged guy local datasheet I think I fried this chip. I was having problems with just the USB connection from the PC. It seemed like the lm35 was drawing too much current such that board couldn't be programmed. You could tell as the 'On' light was very dim. and the L,Tx,Rx lights were never on. I attempted to rectify this situation by concurrently plugging in the 5V power supply. I pulled up the serial monitor and it was (consistently) reporting in the 500F range. I then smelled a burning smell. I unplugged all the power and toched the lm35 and burned myself. I let it cool off and plugged the USB cable back in and the lights on the Arduino board came on. I could connect to the serial monitor but was getting nonsense temperatures. Again I touched the LM35 and it was really hot. I could unplug the jumpers to the LM35 and still get readings. Yeah pretty sure its KIA. I was reading the raw analog value and it was 499-500 when hooked up to 5V so am pretty sure it was just passing through the voltage in 0.1V increments.
